ISAAC WATKINS

Hello Hello! As the text above suggest, my name is Isaac Watkins. I'm a fulltime game dev & entrepreneurial student, as well as a casual coding coach. It's been my dream to create video games and other adjacent forms of media entertainment since 2014, so as such I've developed skills is coding, music, preforming arts, storytelling, 3d modeling, animation, sketching, editing, and 30 or so other odd skills. Creativity, Confidence, and Charisma are three words which start with the letter 'c', and I think that fact alone encapsulates the potential I bring to a team.

Game Connoisseur

Growing up in the 2000s I was lucky enough to be raised at the start of child-demographic video games. Nintendo, Leapfrog, & PvZ Garden Warfare were some of the few games and companies which raised little Isaac, so naturally my abilities to analyze and deconstruct a game into it's common factors are well above average. Additionally, I have well refined taste for all games, with co-operative strategy boardgames like Dead of Winter or Pandemic Legacy, or Gloomhaven being particular favorites. Naturally this applies to digital media of games as well, with indi solo games like Stardew Valley, The Binding of Isaac, and Outerwilds being identity defining games.

Workplace Skills

Now all those activities are well and good, but how do they translate into the workplace?

Starting obvious, a background built upon programming classes as well as a job's worth of experience means that I'm not only an ideal candidate for coding roles of my preferred language, but also incredibly well versed for adapting to new languages and systems.

Less direct but still not out of nowhere, my current course in Game Design, as well as familiarity with Adobe tools such as Photoshop, After Effects, InDesign, Illustrator, Premier Pro, & XD, allow me to design basic to advanced sketches, storyboards, animations, & other creative tools to help with team ideation and visualization.

An underappreciated skill in growing markets, being able to talk a deal or convert a customer is a crucial skill I've got down pat. An art built upon performance, Patience, and being able to make stuff up, my skills have been fine tuned due to years of acting in stage performances, roleplaying TTRPG character, and working in customer service.
